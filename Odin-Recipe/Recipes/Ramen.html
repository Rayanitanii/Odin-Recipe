Ramen

Ingredients

  • Pack of instant noodles
  • Iron Skillet
  • 2 tbsp. onion powder
  • 2 tbsp. ground ginger
  • 2 tbsp. garlic powder
  • Salt, to taste

    • Instructions

    Start off by boiling water in the Iron skillet. Place the ramen in when there are visible bubbles in the water and take out when soft. While the noodles are cooking, get a bowl and insert 2 warm cups of water and the spices listed in the ingredients. When the noodles are finished, place the noodles inside the bowl with the finished broth.
