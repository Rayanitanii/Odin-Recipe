Sunny Side Up Eggs

Ingredients

  • 1 Teaspoon of olive oil
  • 2-4 Chicken Eggs
  • Toast Bread
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Non-stick pan or skillet
  • Salmon(optional)
  • Any object to keep the egg as a circle when in the pan (2-4 depending on how many eggs you are making)

    • Instructions

    For about five minutes on low heat, shimmer the oil in a medium non-stick skillet. Repeat with the second egg, adding it to the opposite side of the skillet. Cover with a tight lid and cook, uninterrupted, until the whites are completely set, but the yolks are still runny, 2 to 2 1/2 minutes. Remove the eggs from the skillet and place them on toast or a dish. Add salt and pepper to taste.
